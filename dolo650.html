💊 Medicine Information: Dolo 650

📌 Basic Information

🧪 Generic Name: Paracetamol (Acetaminophen)

💊 Dosage Form: Tablet

⚖️ Strength: 650 mg

🏭 Manufacturer: Micro Labs

📝 Prescription Required: ❌ No (Over-the-counter)

🩺 Uses / Indications

  • 🌡️ Fever reduction
  • 🧠 Headache relief
  • 💪 Muscle pain
  • 🦷 Toothache
  • 🩸 Post-vaccination fever/pain

⚙️ Mechanism of Action

🧠 Acts on the **central nervous system**

🔧 Inhibits **COX enzymes**, lowering prostaglandin synthesis

🔥 Results in decreased **fever** and **pain perception**

📏 Dosage

👨‍⚕️ Adults: 650 mg every 4–6 hours as needed

🚫 Max 4000 mg/day (do not exceed 6 tablets/day)

❗ Avoid long-term use without medical supervision

⚠️ Side Effects

  • 😐 Nausea
  • 🌀 Dizziness
  • 🩺 Liver toxicity (with overdose or alcohol use)
  • 🤢 Rare allergic reactions (rash, swelling)

🚫 Warnings / Precautions

  • 🍺 Avoid alcohol while using
  • 🩺 Use with caution in liver disease
  • 📋 Do not exceed recommended dose
  • 🧪 Monitor liver function if used regularly

❄️ Storage

🌡️ Store below 25°C

🏖️ Keep in a cool, dry place away from sunlight

🚫 Keep out of reach of children

🔬 Clinical Trials

✅ Well-established antipyretic and analgesic efficacy

🧬 Compared with NSAIDs, it's safer for the stomach

📈 Included in WHO Essential Medicines List

🔄 Drug Interactions

  • 💊 May enhance effects of blood thinners like warfarin
  • ⚠️ Risk of liver damage with alcohol
  • 🔍 Use caution with other hepatotoxic drugs
