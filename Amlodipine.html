💊 Medicine Information: Amlodipine

📌 Basic Information

🧪 Generic Name: Amlodipine

💊 Dosage Form: Tablet

⚖️ Strength: 2.5 mg, 5 mg, 10 mg

🏭 Manufacturer: Pfizer, Sun Pharma, Cipla, etc.

📝 Prescription Required: ✅ Yes

🩺 Uses / Indications

✅ Used to treat:

  • High blood pressure (Hypertension)
  • Angina (Chest pain)
  • Coronary artery disease

⚙️ Mechanism of Action

🧬 Amlodipine is a **calcium channel blocker**.

💉 It relaxes and widens blood vessels, allowing blood to flow more easily, reducing blood pressure and chest pain.

📏 Dosage

👨‍⚕️ Adults:
• Start with 5 mg once daily
• Can be increased to 10 mg depending on response

🕒 Taken once daily, with or without food

⚠️ Side Effects

  • 🦵 Swelling in legs or ankles (edema)
  • 🧠 Dizziness or lightheadedness
  • ❤️ Palpitations
  • 😴 Fatigue
  • 🌡️ Flushing

🚫 Warnings / Precautions

  • 🔬 Monitor blood pressure regularly
  • ⚠️ Use caution in liver impairment
  • 🚗 May cause dizziness – avoid driving if affected
  • 🤰 Use in pregnancy only if prescribed

❄️ Storage

📦 Store at room temperature (15°C – 30°C)

🌥️ Protect from moisture and light

🚫 Keep out of reach of children

🔬 Clinical Trials

📊 Studies have shown Amlodipine effectively reduces blood pressure and improves angina symptoms.

🧪 Trials like ALLHAT supported its use as a first-line treatment for hypertension.

🔄 Drug Interactions

  • ⚠️ May enhance effects of other antihypertensives
  • 🧪 CYP3A4 inhibitors (e.g., clarithromycin) may increase levels
  • 💊 Avoid with grapefruit juice
  • 🧠 Caution with simvastatin (max dose: 20 mg with Amlodipine)
